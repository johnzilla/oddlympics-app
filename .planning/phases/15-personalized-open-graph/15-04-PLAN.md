Recommend documenting this in the Plan 15-04 SUMMARY so future readers know the migration is exhaustive. |
| T-15-17 | Information Disclosure | The `/r/CODE` URL is shared on a public platform → anyone can hit it and trigger a DB lookup | accept | Same threat as Plan 15-01's T-15-04 / T-15-06 — public referral codes are by design observable.
