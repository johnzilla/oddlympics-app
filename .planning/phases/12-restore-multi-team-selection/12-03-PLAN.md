<objective>
Swap the kickoff cron's usersQuery subscription join from
vip_signups.team = teams.slug to a join through the new user_teams table
(D-06), so a user who follows multiple teams gets a kickoff email for any match
involving any of their teams - while NOTIFY-04 (one email per match per channel)
stays guaranteed by the existing match_notifications UNIQUE constraint +
claim-before-send (no new dedupe logic).

Purpose: This is the behavior-critical wiring of the phase - without it, the
multi-team selection a user makes on /manage never affects who gets notified.
It depends only on the user_teams table (12-01), NOT on the /manage UI
(12-02), so it runs in Wave 2 in parallel with 12-02 (zero file overlap).

Output: scripts/send-kickoff-notifications.mjs with the rewritten usersQuery
and an extended defensive DDL block.
</objective>
