## Phase Goal

**As a** site operator about to mass-email a real audience, **I want to** shrink the magic-link replay window from 7 days to 24 hours, **so that** a leaked or forwarded confirmation URL cannot be replayed for a week after the user already confirmed.

<objective>
Drop the magic-link token TTL from 7 days to 24 hours. Single-line change to `TTL_SECONDS` in `src/lib/token.ts:4`, plus the inline comment.

Per D-01, this is **TTL drop only** — no DB-tracked nonce, no `used_tokens` table. Idempotency at the row level (`markConfirmed WHERE confirmed_at IS NULL`) already prevents replay damage on the application side; DB nonce is overkill for v1's risk profile.

Per D-04, no proactive invalidation of in-flight 7-day tokens — `verifyToken` already checks the embedded `exp` field, so legacy tokens age out naturally.

Output: `src/lib/token.ts` with `TTL_SECONDS = 60 * 60 * 24` (was `60 * 60 * 24 * 7`). The comment becomes "24 hours" (was "7 days"). All other crypto and the `verifyToken` flow remain untouched.

Note: this plan deliberately does NOT extend `mintToken`/`verifyToken` with the `purpose` claim. That extension is owned by plan 04 (HARDEN-02 unsubscribe) which depends on this plan finishing first. Splitting keeps the TTL change reviewable in isolation.
</objective>
